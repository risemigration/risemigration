The Genuine Student Requirement

The Australian Student Visa program has shifted to the "Genuine Student" (GS) test. This requires a comprehensive demonstration of your circumstances, the value of the course to your future career, and your incentives to return home or transition to skilled pathways legally.

Our lawyers provide a thorough analysis of your academic and financial background to ensure your GS statement is robust and evidence-backed.
